
# Blinkit Sales Dashboard 

##  Overview

This project analyzes Blinkit’s sales performance using a Power BI dashboard. The focus is on understanding **sales trends, product performance, and outlet contribution**.

---

## Key Metrics

* **Total Sales:** ₹12.0 Lakhs
* **Average Sales:** ₹141
* **Total Items Sold:** 8,523
* **Average Rating:** 3.9 / 5

 The business is performing well with strong revenue, but customer satisfaction can improve.

---

## Product Insights

* **Top Categories:**

  * Fruits & Vegetables (~₹1.8 Lakhs)
  * Snack Foods (~₹1.8 Lakhs)
  * Household (~₹1.4 Lakhs)

* **Lowest Categories:**

  * Seafood, Breakfast (~₹0.1–0.2 Lakhs)

 Daily-use products drive most sales.

---

## Customer Preference

* **Low-fat products:** ~₹7.76 Lakhs
* **Regular products:** ~₹4.25 Lakhs

 ~65% of total sales come from low-fat items → strong health-conscious trend.

---

##  Outlet Performance

### By Outlet Type

* Supermarket Type1: ~₹7.87 Lakhs (highest)
* Others: significantly lower

 Supermarkets dominate revenue.

---

### By Outlet Size

* High: ~₹5.08 Lakhs
* Medium: ~₹4.45 Lakhs
* Small: ~₹2.49 Lakhs

 Larger outlets generate more sales, but medium outlets also perform well.

---

### By Location

* Tier 3: ~₹4.72 Lakhs (highest)
* Tier 2: ~₹3.93 Lakhs
* Tier 1: ~₹3.36 Lakhs

 Tier 3 cities are the strongest market.

---

## Sales Trend

* Growth until **2018 (peak ~₹2.05 Lakhs)**
* Decline after, then stable

 Indicates growth followed by stabilization.

---

## Key Insights

* Strong overall performance with **₹12 Lakhs total sales**
* **Low-fat products dominate** customer preference
* **Fruits, snacks, and household items** drive most revenue
* **Tier 3 cities and large outlets** contribute the most
* Rating (3.9) shows **scope for improvement**

---

##  Recommendations

* Expand **healthy product lines**
* Focus on **top-selling categories**
* Invest more in **Tier 3 markets**
* Improve **customer experience**
